2. About VGAMES

VGAMES partners with OnlineGames.io to bring high-quality games to regions where their services are unavailable or restricted.

Important Note: VGAMES is not a copy of OnlineGames.io. We are an independent service with these key differences:

  • We serve regions OnlineGames.io cannot reach
  • We implement additional security layers
  • We provide localized content and support

Our Security Model

All game links are routed through our safety verification system:

vgames.run.io/safety

This system:

  • Scans for malware and malicious scripts
  • Verifies IP addresses and geographic locations
  • Blocks suspicious traffic before it reaches game servers
  • Cannot be accessed directly by users

IP Protection: We automatically ban IP addresses that:

  • Appear to be spoofed or bogus
  • Originate from known malicious networks
  • Exhibit suspicious behavior patterns

7. Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how VGAMES STUDIO collects, uses, and protects your information in compliance with global data protection regulations.

1. Information Collection

We collect information through multiple channels to ensure service quality and security:

Data Minimization Principle:

We only collect data essential for service operation and user protection.

Directly Provided Data

  • Account Information: Username, encrypted password, recovery email
  • Verification Data: Age verification (month/year only), country/region
  • Payment Processing: Tokenized transactions through PCI-DSS compliant partners

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Fingerprint: Hardware hash (non-PII), OS version, browser type
  • Network Data: IP address (anonymized after 30 days), connection type
  • Security Signals: Request timestamps, TLS handshake metadata

Third-Party Data

  • OnlineGames.io: Game-specific analytics (fully anonymized)
  • Safety Partners: Malware threat intelligence feeds

2. Data Usage

Your information enables these critical functions:

Purpose Legal Basis
Service delivery and maintenance Contractual necessity
Security threat prevention Legitimate interest
Regional content compliance Legal obligation

3. Data Sharing

We implement strict data sharing protocols:

No Sale of Personal Data:

VGAMES STUDIO does not and will never sell user data to third parties.

Essential Service Providers

  • Hosting Partners: Github,AWS, Google Cloud (encrypted data only)
  • Payment Processors: PayPal (tokenized transactions)
  • Security Vendors: Cloudflare, Captcha (threat intelligence)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when legally compelled to:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Law enforcement requests (with proper jurisdiction)
  • Regulatory investigations

4. Data Protection

Our security framework includes:

Technical Measures

  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256)
  • Annual penetration testing
  • Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) for key management

Organizational Measures

  • Employee privacy training (bi-annual)
  • Data Protection Officer oversight
  • Vendor security audits

User Controls

  • Download your data through account settings
  • Request deletion (excluding legally required retention)
  • Opt-out of non-essential processing

Contact Our DPO:

For privacy concerns: dpo@vgames.run.place
Expect response within 72 hours (check spam folder)

10. Account Management

1. Banned Accounts

If your account or IP has been banned, you may appeal by:

  1. Sending an email to vilakazigames@gmail.com
  2. Including your IP address (if known)
  3. Providing details of the issue

Important: Response emails from our system may appear in your spam folder due to:

  • Domain restrictions
  • No-reply implementations
  • Strict spam filtering by some providers

Please check your spam folder if you don't see our response within 48 hours.

2. Account Recovery

For lost accounts, provide:

  • Original email used
  • Approximate creation date
  • Last game played (if known)
