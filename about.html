About TBSV

The story behind the music and the artist

Who I Am

My Journey

Born on 08 May 2007 in Daveyton, Benoni, Gauteng, South Africa, I discovered my passion for music at an early age. Growing up in a vibrant cultural environment, I was exposed to diverse musical influences that shaped my unique sound.

From school performances to professional recordings, my journey has been filled with learning experiences and creative growth.

My Music

My debut album "Tyler" released in 2025 marked the beginning of my professional career. My first single "Good to be bad" received local recognition and motivated me to pursue music more seriously.

I blend contemporary sounds with traditional African rhythms to create a unique musical experience that resonates with diverse audiences.

My Vision

I believe music has the power to unite people and tell stories that matter. My goal is to create authentic, meaningful art that inspires others and represents my heritage with pride.

Through my work, I aim to contribute to the growing African music scene while pushing creative boundaries.
