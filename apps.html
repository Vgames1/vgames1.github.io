How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking a space in the grid, one using X and the other using O. The goal is to be the first to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Example of a Winning Scenario:

In the example above, OX has won by placing three marks diagonally.

Steps to Play:

  1. Player 1 (X) and Player 2 (O) take turns.
  2. Click on an empty cell to place your mark.
  3. The first player to get three of their marks in a row wins.
  4. If all cells are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
